# Sauver Landing Page: Detailed Design Guide

## Aesthetic: "Cinematic Neo-Noir meets digital hope"
- **Style:** Sleek, powerful, trustworthy, urgent. Blade Runner meets luxury brand.
- **Colors:**
  - Backgrounds: Dark charcoal gray, deep midnight blues.
  - Glow/AI: Electrical Cyan, Luminous Gold.
  - Accents: Striking Crimson.
- **Typography:**
  - Headings: Geometric sans-serif (authoritative/futuristic).
  - Body: Clean sans-serif (approachable/human).
- **Motion:** Asymmetrical layouts, parallax scrolling, generous whitespace.

## Section Breakdown

### 1. The Hero (The Banner/Movement Start)
- **Headline:** "Your Attention is the Prize. Your Inbox is the Battlefield. Sauver is Your Shield."
- **Sub-headline:** "They want your data. They steal your time. Stop being the target. Join the movement to reclaim your digital sovereignty."
- **CTA:** "Pick up the Shield"
- **Graphic:** Wide-angle cinematic photo. Lone person on fractured obsidian digital landscape. Cyan energy shield in left hand, Crimson/Gold "Rock" in right. Dark, moody lighting.

### 2. The Enemy (The Problem)
- **Headline:** "The Noise They Created. The Silence You Deserve."
- **Content:** "They’ve weaponized your attention... You are not overwhelmed; you are outnumbered."
- **Graphic:** Abstract chaotic swarm of email icons, notification bubbles, jarring muted colors.

### 3. The Shield (The Protection/Defense)
- **Headline:** "Automated Vanguard. Personalized Defense."
- **Content:** "Sauver is an agent that works only for you... Built on MCP... filters the chaos before it even hits your screen."
- **Graphic:** Geometric technical illustration. Gemini logo + Stylized shield + Orderly color-coded data rows.

### 4. The Rock (The Action/Offense)
- **Headline:** "Don't Just Settle. Fight Back."
- **Content:** "Deflection isn't victory. Sauver empowers you to take action... Every automated action is a rock you throw back."
- **Graphic:** Kinetic motion graphic. Glowing Crimson/Gold "Rock" smashing into a vortex of disjointed text, shattering it into ordered lists.

### 5. The Collective (The Social Proof/Movement)
- **Headline:** "The Silent Majority is Finding Its Voice."
- **Content:** "Digital Sovereignty isn't a technical issue. It's a fundamental right."
- **Graphic:** Composite mosaic. Cyan energy texture with glowing synchronized points of light representing a decentralized network.

### 6. Final CTA
- **Headline:** "The Battle for Your Attention is Over. Claim Your Silence."
- **CTA:** "Pick up the Shield"
- **Graphic:** Minimalist black frame. Centered Sauver shield logo with a single gold pulse ripple.
